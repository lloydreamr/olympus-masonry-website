## The 10 Design Samples

### Sample 1: **Minimal Gold** (Professional)
Clean, professional layout inspired by high-end UK masonry sites. 40% text / 60% image carousels. Gold used sparingly for accents only. Very sophisticated.

### Sample 2: **Bold Gold** (Energetic)
More prominent use of gold with full gold-background sections. Alternating white → gold → black sections. Bolder, larger typography. High-energy, stands out.

### Sample 3: **Dark & Luxe** (Premium)
Mostly dark backgrounds (70-80% black). Gold used as thin accent lines and borders. Extra generous spacing. Premium, sophisticated feel.

### Sample 4: **Modern Grid** (Visual-First)
Pinterest-style masonry grid. Image-focused with minimal text. Hover overlays on images. Modern, clean aesthetic.

### Sample 5: **Split Screen** (Bold)
Fixed 50/50 vertical split throughout entire page. Text always left, images always right. Gold vertical dividing line. Magazine-style, confident.

### Sample 6: **Full-Width Sections** (Editorial)
Full-width sections with varied layouts. Large imagery, magazine/editorial aesthetic. Dynamic, interesting.

### Sample 7: **Card-Based** (Modern)
Everything organized in clean, modern cards with shadows. Grid layouts. Contemporary, well-structured feel.

### Sample 8: **Carousel Hero** (Dynamic)
Full-screen auto-rotating image carousel hero (5 slides). Very visual and engaging. Image slider focus.

### Sample 9: **Video Hero** (Engaging)
Dynamic background in hero section. Modern, premium aesthetic. High-end feel with movement.

### Sample 10: **Minimal Brutalist** (Ultra-Clean)
Extreme whitespace (80% empty space). HUGE typography (150px+ headlines). Geometric gold shapes. Bold, confident, stark.

## What to Consider When Reviewing

As you click through each design, ask yourself:

1. **Which design feels most professional?**
2. **Which layout best showcases your masonry work?**
3. **Which matches how you want customers to see Olympus Masonry?**
4. **Which is easiest to navigate and understand?**
5. **Do you prefer dark or light backgrounds?**
6. **How much gold accent feels right - minimal or bold?**
7. **Do you prefer minimal text or more content?**
8. **Do you like simple layouts or dynamic features (carousels, video)?**
